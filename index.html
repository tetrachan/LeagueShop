About Us

Marc Merrill created Rito Games in order to create an ingame shop in 1907 directly after the great war of league of legends, Dota2, HoTS and Smite. This war later became known as the great War of MoBAs(and one "ARTS"). This war caused Dota2 to become known as an F-ARTS rather than an ARTS since it became free to play, dubbed the "Free-to-play Action Real Time Strategy" game, or FARTS for short.

Then, in 2009, the game league of legends was released in a beta where you could buy items from a shop near your team's fountain, and this is that shop, updated to the latest needs of the consumer throuigh online shopping, especially after amazon took off so well. Marc Merril will consume everything. Throughout the years, we have worked tirelessly on the updating and creation of items for our shop in order to balance gameplay. We take great pride in how perfect our game is balanced and hope you enjoy your experience purchasing items after dying for the eight-teenth time in lane to Mordekaiser ADC.
